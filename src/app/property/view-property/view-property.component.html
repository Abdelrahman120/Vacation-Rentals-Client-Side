About this property

Tiny house on private 20 acres along the Klamath River.

Private cozy space with 500 hundred yards of river front. Copco lake does not have water anymore BUT- the historic Klamath River is back to the valley like it used to be back then! And you got the crazy option to walk on a lake bed and see what is was 100 years ago. On Klamath River you can fish for Rainbow Trout. Property has access to thousands of acres of land lock BLM . You can hunt Deer, Bear, Elk, Turkey, Quail. This place is a paradise for hunters and fisherman's. In winter, you can visit the ski resorts of Ashland and Shasta. Fresh, clean air, the proximity of the river and the forest, comfortable living conditions, convenient location for tourist destination-all this will decorate your holiday in the vicinity of the village at any time of the year. The cabin has everything you need for a comfortable stay. A fool-size futon. a fool - twin size bunk bad, and a kitchen with a refrigerator, microwave, stove and coffee maker. There are two showers , one in the house and one outdoor. Our tiny home is a great place to relax with friends or the whole family!
